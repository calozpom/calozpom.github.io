  • Audio Calls
    Voice quality is the foundation of your offering, and critical to the overall experience.
    We've made sure that the quality of calls is always exceptional.
  • SILK codec integration
    for optimized clarity under hardware and network constraints and real time adjustment to network conditions and CPU resources.
    Web RTC Echo Cancellation
    for real time analysis and removal of unwanted noises generated by the speaker.
    Finely-tuned Jitter buffers
    to provide the perfect balance between packet loss reduction and minimized audio delay.
    Hardened WiFi network handling
    to monitor and adapt to levels of loss in the network, providing dynamic loss correction and suppression.
  • Video Calls
    Now users can access video calling without moving to a competing application and without the barrier of their enterprise investing in expensive equipment.
  • Move Calls
    For a truly unified experience, calls can be moved seamlessly between devices.

  • Call Manager
    3 simple settings to decide how incoming calls are handled. Set to Do Not Disturb if you're unavailable, or forward all your calls if you're out of the office without data access.
  • Visual Voicemail
    No more navigating through TUIs - Visual Voicemail gives you instant access to your mailbox. Tap on a message to listen to it, or just read the transcription if you're stuck in a meeting.
  • Videomail
    Videomail provides all the benefits a video call does for your enterprise users - a great opportunity to upsell from basic video calling.
